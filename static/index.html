So what is BDD? (1/2)

  • an agile software development technique
  • second generation of TDD
  • aims to bring developers and non-technical stakeholders closer together

So what is BDD? (2/2)

  • originally coined in 2003 by Dan North
  • evolved from an extension of TDD
  • critics say it's just a fancy word for TDD
  • no chanonical definition!
